-----INGREDIENTS-----
--bread--
1   pkg yeast
1/8 cup warm water
3/4 cup buttermilk 
1/4 cup vegetable oil
9/4 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup butter (melted)
5/8 cup brown sugar
1   tsp cinnamon (ground)

--icing--
1   cup powdered sugar
8   oz cream cheese
4   tbs butter
1/2 tsp vanilla

-----INSTRUCTIONS-----
oven temp 400  F

prepare yeast and warm water, let yeastie beasties grow big and strong
heat buttermilk until warm to the touch

combine flour, salt, and baking soda
mix buttermilk, oil, and yeast into a bowl

gradually stir in flour mixture
knead dough ~20 times
cover and let rest 15 minutes

in a small bowl stir together butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon

roll dough out into a rectangle
spread cinnamon mixture on the dough
roll into a log

slice into 1 inch pieces and arrange pieces on a lightly greased pan
cover and let rise 30 minutes

bake ~20 minutes

beat icing ingredients until smooth
spread icing on finished rolls
