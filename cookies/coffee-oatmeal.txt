-----INGREDIENTS-----
1   cup flour
1   cup oats
1   tsp baking soda
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1   tsp vanilla
1   tsp cinnamon
1   large egg
1/4 cup coffee (finely ground)


-----INSTRUCTIONS-----
oven temp 375 F

combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and oats

mix butter, sugar, brown sugar until creamy
add in egg and vanilla
add in coffee
gradually add dry ingredients

bake ~10 minutes
cool on wire rack
