-----INGREDIENTS-----
--cake--
2   cups flour
1   cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2   tsp baking powder
1   tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmet
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp all spice
1/2 tsp cloves
1/2 tsp ginger
2   large eggs
5/4 cup milk
2   tsp vanilla
3/2 tsp oil

--coating--
3/2 cup sugar
3/2 tsp cinnamon
3/4 cup oil


-----INSTRUCTIONS-----
oven temp 350 F

combine dry ingredients
whisk eggs + liquids
gradually add dry ingredients
fill mold 3/4 full
bake ~17 minutes
cool completely

coat with oil
toss doughnut in cinnamon sugar mix
