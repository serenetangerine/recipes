# Apple Empanadas 
> Author: mattie
>
> Version: 1.0
>
> Modified:


## INGREDIENTS
```
2   cups flour
2   egg yolks
1   whole egg
1   tbs sugar
1/2 cup water
1/2 tsp salt
3   apples
1/2 cup brown sugar
1   tbs cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
powdered sugar
```


## INSTRUCTIONS

**oven temp 350 F**

```
-- filling --
peel and dice apples
cook apples, sugars, cinnamon, and nutmeg on medium heat ~ 5-7 minutes until apples are soft and translucent
sprinkle with powdered sugar


-- dough --
mix 2 egg yolks, salt, and water
knead in flour
let set 10 - 15 minutes

roll out dough to ~ 1/8"
cut dough into 4" circles
add ~ 2 tbs filling
fold and crimp

whisk egg with splash of water for egg wash
brush until evenly coated

bake ~ 8-10 minutes

dust powdered sugar
```
