# Apple Cider Bundt Cake
> Author: name
>
> Version: 1.0
>
> Modified:


## INGREDIENTS
```
---cake---
3   cups flour
2   tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1   tsp salt
3/2 tbs baking powder
3/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1   cup vegetable oil
3   eggs
1   cup applesauce
1   cup apple cider (not vinegar)
1   tsp vanilla

---topping---
1/4 cup butter (melted)
1/4 cup sugar
2   tsp cinnamon
```


## INSTRUCTIONS

**oven temp 325 F**

```
---cake---
spray bundt pan w/ non-stick spray and dust w/ flour

in a separate bowl combine flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and baking powder

mix sugar, brown sugar, and oil
add eggs
add applesauce and vanilla

add 1/3 flour mixture
add 1/2 apple cider
repeat until depleted (batter should be slightly thin, not thick)

add batter to bundt pan and bake ~50 minutes
cake is done when toothpick has a few moist crumbs

let cool ~1 hour in pan before turning over to cooking rack

coat cake in butter w/ pastry brush
combine topping sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle on top and sides of cake
```
